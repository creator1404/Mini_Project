About Me

We are a team passionate about making communication easier for everyone, especially for those in the deaf and hard of hearing community. Our goal is to break down the barriers of communication using technology to promote inclusivity.




Vision:




The Sign Language Decoder website aims to bridge communication gaps by offering accessible, real-time sign language translation. It empowers users, promotes inclusivity, raises awareness, and provides educational resources to foster understanding and seamless communication for the deaf and hard-of-hearing community.
